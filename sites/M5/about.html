M5 Showroom has integrated an impressive compilation of international labels into the North American market over the past 15 years, Moncler, Belstaff and Stone Island to name a few. This impact has been informed by our team’s approach to luxury retail and distinct point of view on go to market strategies. With the same intimate and informed approach, M5 Showroom presents the M5 Shop, a 24-hour concept space offering an unmatched style of expertise and customer service, backed with a premium shopping experience; booked by appointment only.

The team behind the Shop is made up of the Showroom’s brand representatives, who lead the curation of product and culture on a daily basis. Their knowledge along with their appreciation for quality and provenance illuminates the 3,500 square-foot space, as they pay homage to the brands championing modern sportswear and cross-generational client base.

Grounded in a common affinity for classic luxury, innovative sportswear and personal style, M5 evolves menswear with this unique shopping experience and atmosphere. The initial brands offered will be Stone Island, Herno, Begg & Co., Barena, TS(S), President’s, and Pierre Louis Mascia. M5 Shop will be open between market seasons from April-June (offering Spring/Summer collections) and Oct-Dec (offering Fall/Winter collections).
